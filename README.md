## Project ANCHOR: Reclaiming Independence

**"Your neighborhood, always within reach."** 

**Project ANCHOR** is an intelligent, haptic wearable companion designed specifically for active seniors ($60+$) who wish to explore their local neighborhoods independently and without fear. Rather than acting as a restrictive medical monitor, it serves as a "quiet companion" that learns familiar routes over time. It provides gentle guidance only when necessary, fostering a sense of freedom and belonging for users.
